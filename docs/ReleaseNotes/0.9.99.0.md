## Change Summary

* NEW 3.75m (inner diameter) drop tanks
  * TechRequired = advFuelSystems
* English and Dutch localizations
* Compatibility
  * [B9FuelSwitch.cfg]
  * [InterstellarFuelSwitch.cfg]
  * [TweakScale.cfg]
* Subject to change (K5 need input!!!)
  * mass
  * cost/entryCost
  * capacity
  * Decoupler: explosiveForce (eF)
